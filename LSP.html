Professional Bio

Hi! I'm Leah Yates. A bachelor of Social Work student at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas, graduating in May 2025. Throughout my academic journey, I've remained passionate about macro social work, with a focus on community empowerment, systems change, and social justice. My field experience at Skills 4 Kids and Serenity Mental Health have strengthened my understanding of case management and  mental health services, while also motivating me to work towards improving current current policies that impact people's lives. I'm especially interested in advocating for equitable resources, developing community-based programs,  and shaping policies that support long-term change. This portfolio should highlight the work I've done and the foundation I've built as I prepare for a career dedicated to creating a meaningful impact.
